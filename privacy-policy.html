Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Edwin Meiteikini ("I", "me", "my") operates the website meiteikiniedwin.tech (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect information when you visit my Website, in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and international best practices including GDPR.

2. Information I Collect

Analytics Data (with your consent):

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level only)
  • IP addresses are anonymized before storage

Contact Form Submissions:

  • Name, email address, and message content you voluntarily provide through the contact form
  • This data is processed by Web3Forms and sent to my email

Information I do NOT collect:

  • I do not collect passwords, financial information, or sensitive personal data
  • I do not use tracking cookies for advertising or marketing purposes
  • I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties

3. How I Use Your Information

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use my website and improve content and performance
  • Contact Form: To respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Website Improvement: To identify and fix technical issues

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website uses the following:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Only loaded after you accept cookies. Uses first-party cookies to measure website traffic. IP anonymization is enabled. Data is processed by Google under their Privacy Policy.
  • localStorage: Used to remember your cookie consent preference. This stays on your device and is not sent to any server.

No advertising cookies, social media trackers, or third-party marketing tools are used on this website.

5. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Consent: Choose whether to accept or decline analytics cookies
  • Withdraw Consent: Change your cookie preferences at any time using the "Cookie Preferences" button on the website
  • Access: Request information about what data has been collected about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any personal data I hold
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the details below.

6. Data Retention

  • Analytics data: Retained by Google Analytics for 14 months, then automatically deleted
  • Contact form data: Retained in my email inbox until the inquiry is resolved, then deleted
  • Cookie consent preference: Stored in your browser's localStorage until you clear it or change your preference

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Website traffic analysis — Google Privacy Policy
  • Web3Forms: Contact form processing — Web3Forms Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps: Embedded map on the contact page — Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting — GitHub Privacy Statement

8. Data Security

This website is served over HTTPS to ensure data is encrypted in transit. I take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 18. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.
